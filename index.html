Popular Services

Individual Coaching Therapy

Cia's approach combines life coaching and body-centered (somatic) therapy, with traditional methods such as DBT to address mental health concerns, ensuring a holistic therapeutic experience. Individual sessions are available via phone or Zoom.

Couples Therapy

Choose to grow as you learn from Cia’s body centered healing, and welcome new perspectives into your relationship. Cia’s couples sessions are available in person or via Zoom.

Retreats

Choose from a list of suggested topics or bring your own, attend locally in Florida, or have Cia travel to your group’s location. Cia’s group workshops are fully customizable to meet your needs.

Support Groups

Cia offers virtual support groups via Zoom. You can invite your friends and model your sessions after past examples, such as “Living, Aging, and Dying,” a support group focused on senescence. You can also create your own group with Cia’s help for a more tailored fit.

Individual Intensives

Cia’s individual intensives range from 3 days up to a month. These sessions are flexible: Cia can travel to your location or you can stay with her for a more personalized experience.

Cia's Expertise

40+ Years Experience

Most importantly, Cia believes in learning by doing. Her extensive hands-on experience has been instrumental in developing her unique therapeutic methods. Cia Ricco has over four decades of experience in body-centered psychotherapy, helping countless individuals on their journey to healing and growth.

Teaching

Cia has taught extensively at the Kripalu Center for Yoga & Health, sharing her expertise with a wide audience. She has also led workshops at other holistic centers for growth, including the Omega Institute, the Wainwright House, and the New York Open Center.

Studies

Cia has studied under pioneers in the field of somatic therapy, including the renowned Gay and Kathlyn Hendricks at the Hendricks Institute. She has attended embodiment workshops led by Douglas Brady and studied Robert Gass’s curriculum at Springhill, and is trained in Hakomi therapy—the precursor to modern somatic therapeutic approaches—along with other materials by Ron Kurtz.

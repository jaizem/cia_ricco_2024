Cia on body centered therapy, also known as somatic healing.

Body-centered therapy, also known as somatic healing, is a powerful approach to psychotherapy that integrates the mind and body to address trauma and promote healing. Cia Ricco explains, "Our bodies hold onto past traumas and emotions. By reconnecting with our physical selves, we can release these stored experiences and achieve a deeper sense of peace and well-being." This method allows individuals to process and release stored trauma, fostering self-awareness and holistic healing.

What is Somatic Therapy?

What is the Benefit to Body Centered Healing?

Somatic therapy is a gentle yet profound approach that honors the deep connection between our minds and bodies. It acknowledges that our bodies hold onto past experiences and traumas, and through mindful awareness and therapeutic techniques, it helps release these stored emotions. This holistic method promotes healing by encouraging us to listen to our bodies and embrace the wisdom they hold.

How is a Heart-Centered Approach Different?

A heart-centered approach is rooted in compassion and empathy. It focuses on creating a safe and nurturing space where you can feel truly seen and heard. By fostering a deep sense of connection and understanding, this approach helps you connect with your authentic self, facilitating profound emotional healing and personal growth.

What to Expect from Your Work with Cia?

Working with Cia Ricco is a deeply transformative experience. With over 40 years of experience, Cia combines somatic and heart-centered techniques to guide you on your healing journey. You can expect a personalized approach that respects your unique needs, incorporating body-centered exercises, mindfulness practices, and compassionate dialogue. Cia's wisdom and empathy create a supportive environment where you can explore, heal, and grow.

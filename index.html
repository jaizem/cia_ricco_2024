Cia Ricco

Therapist, Teacher, and Author

Cia on body centered therapy, also known as somatic healing.

Body-centered therapy, also known as somatic healing, is a powerful approach to psychotherapy that integrates the mind and body to address trauma and promote healing. Cia Ricco explains, "Our bodies hold onto past traumas and emotions. By reconnecting with our physical selves, we can release these stored experiences and achieve a deeper sense of peace and well-being." This method allows individuals to process and release stored trauma, fostering self-awareness and holistic healing.

Popular Services

Individual Intensives

Cia’s individual intensives range from 3 days up to a month. These sessions are flexible: Cia can travel to your location or you can stay with her for a more personalized experience.

Group Workshops

Choose from a list of suggested topics or bring your own, attend locally in Florida, or have Cia travel to your group’s location. Cia’s group workshops are fully customizable to meet your needs.

Support Groups

Join open support groups such as Living, Aging, and Dying which offers support for senescence, or create your own support group with Cia’s help for a more tailored fit.

Couples Therapy

Choose to grow as you learn from Cia’s body centered healing, and welcome new perspectives into your relationship. Cia’s couples sessions are available in person or via Zoom.

Individual Therapy

Cia's approach combines life coaching with somatic healing to address mental health concerns, ensuring a holistic therapeutic experience. Individual sessions are available via phone, Zoom, or in person.

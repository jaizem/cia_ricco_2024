Embark on a transformative journey that combines body-centered therapeutic techniques with guided meditation, movement, breathwork, and optional hallucinogenic substances. This retreat offers a unique opportunity to enhance your personal growth, empowerment, and connection to the universe.

Leave the retreat with greater self-awareness, healing, and a renewed connection to the natural world, others, and the divine.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the retreat schedule?

The retreat schedule will be provided upon registration and includes daily activities such as meditation, breathwork sessions, and group discussions. A detailed itinerary will be available closer to the retreat date.

Q: What is included in the retreat cost?

The retreat cost includes accommodation, meals, all scheduled activities, and access to amenities at The Manor at Bethel Landing. Optional hallucinogenic substances are available at an additional cost.

Q: How do I get to the retreat location?

The Manor at Bethel Landing is located in Bethel, NY. The nearest airport is Stewart International Airport (SWF) which is 48 miles away. Detailed driving directions will be provided upon registration. Transportation from the airport can be arranged upon request.

Q: What should I bring to the retreat?

We recommend bringing comfortable clothing, personal toiletries, a journal, and any medications you may need. A full packing list will be provided upon registration.

Q: Are the hallucinogenic substances safe?

The optional hallucinogenic substances used during the retreat are administered in a controlled, supportive environment by experienced professionals. Safety and well-being are our top priorities.

Q: Can I attend the retreat if I have dietary restrictions?

Yes, we accommodate various dietary restrictions. Please inform us of your dietary needs when you register so we can ensure appropriate meal options are available.

Q: Is there a cancellation policy?

Yes, we have a cancellation policy. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact us directly for more information.

Q: What is the accommodation like?

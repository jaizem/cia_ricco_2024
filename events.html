Retreat Details

Experience a transformative retreat in the beautiful Pan Sand Resort, Thailand. Our retreat will combine therapeutic techniques with guided meditation, movement, breathwork, and the opportunity to explore the stunning surroundings.

  • Enjoy kayaking, snorkeling, and other water adventures.

FAQs

Q: What types of events does Cia Ricco offer?

Cia Ricco offers a variety of events including quarterly retreats, group therapy sessions, and public meet and greets. Each event is designed to provide unique and valuable experiences.

Q: How can I register for an upcoming event?

You can register for an upcoming event by contacting us directly via phone or email. Please refer to the registration section for contact details.

Q: What should I bring to a retreat?

For retreats, we recommend bringing comfortable clothing, personal toiletries, a journal, and any medications you may need. A detailed packing list will be provided upon registration.

Q: Are there any costs associated with attending events?

Yes, there are costs associated with attending events. Prices vary depending on the type of event. Detailed pricing information will be provided during the registration process.

Q: Can I attend events if I have dietary restrictions?

Yes, we accommodate various dietary restrictions. Please inform us of your dietary needs when you register so we can ensure appropriate meal options are available.

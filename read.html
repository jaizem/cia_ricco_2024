Living As If Your Life Depended On It!

With insight born of personal and professional experience, Cia Ricco guides us, gently and lovingly, through the obstacles that hinder so many lives. The twelve gateways will reveal and transform the mechanisms which separate you from your life! Each gateway presents the unlimited opportunities for personal discovery and growth. Like having a dozen books integrated into one, what you hold in your hands is an encyclopedia of concise wisdom for transforming your career, your relationships, your health -- your entire life.

Reviews for "Living As If Your Life Depended On It!"

Loveetwar: ★★★★★ "A female warrior shows her path to change and living life directly." In this book, an experienced therapist who apparently applied what she learned on herself as well as her clients gives her road map to taking responsibility for one's life and creating the life one wants. Cia Ricco gives her path to living your life as if your life depended on it, which of course it does, but few recognize that and few live their lives accordingly. The language is simple and direct and this might lead one to conclude that it is too simple to be effective. That would be a grave mistake. With years of practice as a therapist and in Buddhist meditation, the author gently guides the reader through 12 gateways. How effective they become is totally up to the reader, but there is a powerful tool here for those who work on themselves and wish to live life more directly.

Wonder: ★★★★★ "What a simple, yet profound book." I find myself coming back to it again and again. As I take in its wisdom, deeper and deeper, others around me seem to appreciate it as well. Cia has a way of making what we see as complicated, very simple and straightforward. Her message is real and authentic. I have noticed many shifts in my life because of this book. For that I am grateful.

Rachel Cohen: ★★★★★ "One of the best self-help books out there." It's clear and concise, easy to read. If you integrate what's offered your life will be transformed!

David Mullins: ★★★★★ "A Small, Often Overlooked Book That Is TRULY Life-Changing." Cia Ricco's writing in 'Living As If Your Life Depended on It' is direct and cuts through the clutter of psycho-babble of the many degreed therapists, psychiatrists, and psychologists, and combines their valid, but often abstract, observations into instantly practical advice. She believes that repetition of mental and verbal story-telling keep many people in the same rut, and works to go from 'A' to 'B' by the most direct path. This book is VERY special, but not well-publicized as I understand Ms. Ricco has re-located part-time to Costa Rica. I have seen it positively affect readers from a teenager to an elderly 86-year-old woman!

Fro: ★★★★★ "Your life belongs to YOU!" Cia Ricco's book is simple and profound at the same time. It has 12 gateways, each of which has the potential to open you up to your full potential. This book may help you shift your beliefs, improve your self-talk and motivate you to take control of your life- after all, your life belongs to YOU! It's time you started living that way.

Amazon Customer: ★★★★★ "Stepping into MY life!" Cia's work has helped me understand that this life we have is NOT a dress rehearsal...that we only go around once and in this life we are each entitled to our full measure of happiness and abundance. I choose to be present in my life everyday...and I will be forever grateful that I chose this book.

Jean Quat: ★★★★★ "A Wonderful Way to Heal Your Life!" This book is sweet and wise and user-friendly. It can really make a practical difference in your life on every level. Very helpful and inspiring!
